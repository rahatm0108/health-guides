Why it’s important to improve your physical health?

Physical activity or exercise can improve your health and reduce the risk of developing several diseases like type 2 diabetes, cancer and cardiovascular disease. Physical activity and exercise can have immediate and long-term health benefits. Most importantly, regular activity can improve your quality of life.

Benefits of being physically healthy:


-reduce your risk of a heart attack

-manage your weight better

-have a lower blood cholesterol level

-lower the risk of type 2 diabetes and some cancers

-have lower blood pressure

-have stronger bones, muscles and joints

-feel better

–more energy, a better mood, feel more relaxed and sleep better.

Ways to improve your physical health:

Drink More Water Because the body is primarily composed of water, staying hydrated is crucial to your physical health. Staying properly hydrated helps the body's cells to function more efficiently. Exercise regularly Take a walk, ride a bike, or jog around the neighborhood. Push-ups, sit-ups, leg lifts, lunges, and stretching go a long way toward improving your health. Getting off the couch to play a sport or go on a hike as a family creates good habits and a healthier you. Eat more fruits and vegetables rather than unhealthy foods Fruits and vegetables contain natural vitamins and minerals your body needs to function optimally. Snack on apples instead of chips, enjoy a carrot instead of french fries.
